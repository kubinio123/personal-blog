Tailwind works best when you standardize recurring UI patterns.

For landing pages, keep spacing, typography, and card styles consistent:
## TEST

- section wrappers with predictable vertical rhythm,
- balanced max-width constraints,
- and accessible color contrast in every state.

This reduces design drift and speeds up iteration.
